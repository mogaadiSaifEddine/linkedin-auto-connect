How It Works

Getting started with LinkedIn Auto Connect is quick and easy. Follow these simple steps:

1

Install the Extension

Add LinkedIn Auto Connect to your Chrome browser from the Chrome Web Store with just one click.

2

Navigate to LinkedIn

Go to LinkedIn's "My Network" page or any search results page with potential connections.

3

Configure Settings

Click on the extension icon and set your preferences for maximum connections, delay time, and connection notes.

4

Start Connecting

Click the "Start Auto Connect" button and watch as the extension automatically grows your network for you.

5

Monitor Progress

Keep track of your connection requests with real-time status updates in the extension popup.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about LinkedIn Auto Connect.

Is this extension safe to use with my LinkedIn account?

LinkedIn Auto Connect is designed to mimic natural human behavior by including reasonable delays between actions. However, LinkedIn does have limits on connection requests (approximately 100 per week), so we recommend using the extension responsibly and staying within these limits to avoid any account restrictions.

Does the extension collect my data?

No. LinkedIn Auto Connect operates entirely within your browser and does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or LinkedIn credentials to external servers.

How many connection requests can I send?

The extension allows you to set your own limit for connection requests. However, we recommend staying within LinkedIn's guidelines of approximately 100 connection requests per week to avoid any account restrictions.

Can I customize connection request messages?

The current version is designed to send requests without notes for maximum efficiency. Support for customized connection messages is planned for a future update.

Which LinkedIn pages does the extension work on?

LinkedIn Auto Connect works on any LinkedIn page that displays connect buttons, including "My Network" suggestions, search results pages, alumni pages, and "People You May Know" sections.

Is this extension approved by LinkedIn?

LinkedIn Auto Connect is an independent tool and is not officially affiliated with or endorsed by LinkedIn. It is designed to enhance your LinkedIn experience while respecting LinkedIn's platform.
